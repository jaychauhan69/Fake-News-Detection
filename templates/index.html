How it works

01
Text cleaning NLTK removes stopwords, URLs, and noise, then lemmatizes each token.
02
TF-IDF vectorisation 15,000-feature unigram + bigram + trigram vectors capture language patterns.
03
Logistic Regression Trained on 64,252 balanced articles from WELFake + ISOT + Indian News datasets.
04
Image Forensics EXIF metadata, ELA, noise analysis, JPEG artifacts, histogram, and lighting consistency checks.
